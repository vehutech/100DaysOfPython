Our Story

Founded by Nonna Maria, Grandma's Restaurant began as a small trattoria in Little Italy, bringing her cherished family recipes to the community. Over the decades, we've grown while staying true to our roots, offering dishes made with love and tradition.

Our commitment to quality ingredients and time-honored techniques has made us a beloved destination for locals and visitors alike.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Antonio Rossi, Nonna Maria's grandson, leads our kitchen with passion and expertise. Trained in Italy and inspired by his grandmother's recipes, Antonio brings innovation to classic dishes while preserving their authentic flavors.

His dedication to sustainable sourcing and seasonal ingredients ensures every plate tells a story of tradition and care.
